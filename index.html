Common
Questions

Can't find what you are looking for? Reach out to our support team directly via the contact portal for immediate assistance.

Are these jerseys player version or fan version?

+

We source both. Player version jerseys feature a slim, athletic cut with heat-pressed badges and advanced ventilation fabrics. Fan versions offer a slightly standard, relaxed fit with beautifully embroidered club crests. The specific version is always noted in the item details.

How long does delivery take within Lagos?

+

Orders within Lagos (Island and Mainland) typically deliver within 24 to 48 hours. For standard nationwide shipping outside Lagos, please allow 3 to 5 business days for your package to arrive.

Can I customize a jersey with my own name and number?

+

Yes. Custom nameplates and numbers use official club fonts and pairing graphics. To request personalization, select the custom option on the product page or drop your specific instructions in the checkout notes.

What is your return policy if a jersey doesn't fit?

+

We accept exchanges within 7 days of delivery, provided the tags are completely intact and the item is unworn. Please note that custom personalized jerseys (with custom names/numbers) cannot be returned or exchanged.
