Our Story

Every kit
has a
story.

It started with a Bayern Munich home shirt from 2001, found tucked in a secondhand market in Lagos. Clean stitching. Original badge. The kind of piece you don't walk past.

That single find became an obsession tracking down authentic match-worn and retro kits from Europe's greatest clubs. Not replicas. Not knock-offs. The real thing, for people who know the difference.

PLUGONJERSEYS. was built on the belief that football history deserves to live on in wardrobes. Every piece we carry is hand checked, authenticated, and top notch quality.
